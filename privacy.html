privacy policy.

what we collect

If you sign up for the free Processing 101 lessons, we collect your name and email address. That's it. Submissions are delivered to our inbox so we can send you the lessons and occasional related emails.

The rate check tool runs entirely in your browser. The numbers you type are never sent anywhere — not to us, not to anyone.

what we do with it

We use your email to send the lessons you asked for and occasional updates about Targeted Market. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing.

opting out

Every email includes a way out. Reply "remove" to any email from us and you're off the list, same day, permanently. Once removed, we won't re-add you.

third parties we use

GitHub Pages hosts this site (standard server logs apply). Whop handles program checkout and payments — we never see your card number. Google Calendar powers the booking link. Each has its own privacy policy that applies when you use it.

sensitive information

Do not send us bank account details, card numbers, Social Security numbers, or completed merchant applications through this site or by email. Merchant paperwork is handled only through secure, approved processing systems.

deletion

Want your info deleted? Reply "delete my data" to any email from us, or submit the form on the homepage with that request. We'll confirm when it's done.
